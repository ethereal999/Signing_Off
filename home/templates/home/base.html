Signing off is an initiative to pass on feasible objects to the newbies who need them, along with messages from the seniors who once walked the same road they are about to tread on. We collect bicycles and mattresses from the graduating seniors and pass them on to the incoming batch of juniors with the help of the Welfare Board, IIT Guwahati.

Simple idea, right? But look how it impacts a freshman's life. We nudge them onto this beautiful journey, with new aspirations, new dreams but some old and cherished memories.
